How We Design Learning

We combine ADDIE, Agile methods, and Adult Learning Principles.

Analyze → Design → Develop → Implement → Evaluate

  • Analyze: We identify your performance gaps and learner needs to ensure the solution targets the right business problems.
  • Design: We map out the learning journey, creating blueprints and storyboards that prioritize engagement and instructional flow.
  • Develop: Our team builds the high-fidelity content, transforming designs into interactive, mobile-ready, and accessible digital assets.
  • Implement: We handle the technical heavy lifting, from SCORM/xAPI integration to a seamless rollout across your LMS.
  • Evaluate: We analyze learner data and feedback to measure impact and continuously optimize the content for better results.

This ensures learning is:

  • Purpose-driven
  • Engaging and easy to follow
  • Continuously improved based on results
